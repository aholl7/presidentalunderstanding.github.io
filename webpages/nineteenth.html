21st Century Presidents

1: George Washington

Presidential Party:

Independent

Years Served:

1789-1797

Vice President:

John Adams

Accomplishments:

  1. Signed into law the Copyright Act of 1790
  2. Proclaimed November 26 as Thanksgiving National Holiday
  3. Personally led troops into field to stop the Whiskey Rebellion
  4. Vetoed on two bills during his presidency : The Apportionment Bill & another involving the military
  5. His second inaugural address is the shortest ever delivered in history
