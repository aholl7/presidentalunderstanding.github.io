21st Century Presidents

43: George W. Bush

Presidential Party:

Republican

Years Served:

2001 - 2009

Vice President:

Dick Cheney

Accomplishments:

  1. Signed the Patriot Act into law which created the Cabinet-level Department of Homeland Security
  2. Signed the No Child Left Behind Act into law which authorized several federal education programs that are administered by the states
  3. Created the White House Office of Faith-Based and Community Initiatives
  4. Made two nominations (Chief Justice John Roberts & Samuel Alito) to the U.S. Supreme Court
  5. Invaded Iraq following 9/11 and captured Sadaam Hussein

44: Barack Obama

Presidential Party:

Democratic

Years Served:

2009 - 2017

Vice President:

Joe Biden

Accomplishments:

  1. Signed the Affordable Care Act into law that provided insurance to over 20 million Americans who were uninsured
  2. Oversaw the elimination of Osama bin Laden
  3. Ended the war in Iraq
  4. Signed the Hate Crimes Prevention Act into law which made it a federal crime to assault anyone based on sexuality or gender identification
  5. Repealed Don't Ask Don't Tell which, after the repeal had taken effect, allowed those apart of the LGBT community to serve openly within the Armed Forces

45: Donald J. Trump

Presidential Party:

Republican

Years Served:

2017 - 2021

Vice President:

Mike Pence

Accomplishments:

  1. Signed the Tax Cuts and Jobs Act into law which amended the Internal Revenue Code of 1986
  2. Established the Governors’ Initiative on Regulatory Innovation to reduce outdated regulations at every level
  3. Established the White House Coronavirus Task Force to manage the Administration’s efforts to mitigate the spread of COVID-19
  4. Signed the Families First Coronavirus Relief Act into law which guaranteed items such as free coronavirus testing and emergency paid sick and family leave
  5. Oversaw the elimination of Abu Bakr al-Baghdadi and Qasem Soleimani

46: Joe Biden (Current)

Presidential Party:

Democratic

Years Served:

2021 - Present

Vice President:

Kamala Harris

Accomplishments:

  1. Signed the American Rescue Plan Act of 2021 into law which aimed to speed up the United States' recovery from the effects of the COVID-19 pandemic and the ongoing recession
  2. Rejoined the Paris Climate Agreement
  3. Signed an executive order requiring masks on federal property
  4. Rejoined the World Health Organization
  5. Signed the most executive orders out of most recent presidents in his first 100 days
